Detection Box - Assisting Night Driving with Infrared Camera
This is a small box that uses the YOLO model to perform real-time detection of pedestrians, vehicles, and other objects in front of the vehicle, especially in low visibility conditions such as nighttime or heavy fog. It assists drivers by displaying the results using HUD (a display technology). The box achieves a speed of over 150 frames per second and maintains an accuracy rate of over 50%.
